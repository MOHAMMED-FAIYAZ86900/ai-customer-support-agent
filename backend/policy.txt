REFUND POLICY

1. Refund requests must be made within 30 days of purchase.

2. Product must be delivered before refund can be processed.

3. Digital products are non-refundable.

4. Refund amount must not exceed $1000.

5. Only one refund per order is allowed.

6. A valid order ID is required.

7. Orders marked as fraudulent are not eligible for refund.
